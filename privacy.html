Privacy Policy

> LAST UPDATED: JANUARY 19, 2026

This updated Privacy Policy has been consolidated to cover your entire suite of apps: AudioBrief, Telugu News App, and Current Affairs 2026. We have streamlined the language to ensure compliance with 2026 standards for GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), and the DPDP Act (India).

[ INFORMATION ]

This Privacy Policy applies to the following mobile applications (collectively referred to as "the Apps") developed and published by 8bitlog:

  • AudioBrief
  • Telugu News App
  • Current Affairs 2026

At 8bitlog, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This document explains how we handle information when you use our services.

[ 1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ]

The Apps are designed to function without requiring you to create an account or provide personal details.

> NO PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION (PII)

We do not collect, store, or request your name, email address, phone number, or precise GPS location.

> NO SENSITIVE DATA

We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone (unless explicitly used for search in supported versions), or files.

> ON-DEVICE DATA

Preferences (such as bookmarked articles or language settings) are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

[ 2. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES ]

We partner with trusted third-party providers to maintain app performance and monetize our services. These partners may collect non-personal, pseudonymized data:

> GOOGLE ADMOB

Collects device identifiers (Android Advertising ID) and approximate location to serve relevant ads.

> FIREBASE ANALYTICS

Provides aggregated, anonymous insights into how users interact with the Apps (e.g., most-read categories).

> FIREBASE CRASHLYTICS

Collects technical logs (device model, OS version) to help us fix bugs and crashes.

> GOOGLE PLAY BILLING

Processes in-app purchases. We never see or store your credit card or banking details.

[ 3. GLOBAL PRIVACY COMPLIANCE ]

>> INDIA: DPDP ACT (2023/2026)

We comply with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act.

  • We act as a Data Fiduciary only for the minimal technical data required to run the Apps.
  • Since no PII is collected by 8bitlog, we do not share or sell Indian users' personal data.

>> EUROPEAN UNION: GDPR

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage consent.

  • You can choose between Personalized and Non-Personalized ads.
  • Consent can be withdrawn at any time via the "Privacy Settings" or "Manage Consent" menu within the Apps.

>> CALIFORNIA: CCPA/CPRA

California residents have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. While we do not sell data for money, the use of advertising identifiers is considered "sharing." You may opt-out via your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

[ 4. PERMISSIONS REQUESTED ]

To provide our services, the Apps may request the following permissions:

  • INTERNET: To fetch the latest news and audio content.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To verify if you are connected to Wi-Fi or Mobile Data.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE (AudioBrief only): To allow audio playback to continue while you use other apps or lock your screen.

[ 5. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY ]

Our Apps are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

[ 6. DATA SECURITY ]

We utilize industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transmission between the Apps and our content servers, ensuring that your news feed and app interactions remain secure from interception.

[ 7. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY ]

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy and via a notice within the Apps.
